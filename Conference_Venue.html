Conference Venue : Wanda Vista Shenyang(沈阳富力万达文华酒店)

Address:No.17-5 Yingpan West Street, ,Hunnan, Shenyang, Liaoning Province(沈阳市浑南区营盘西街17-5号) 

Transportation to the Venue

Shenyang Railway station

1. Take a taxi: 11.4 km from the hotel, approximately 35 minutes.
2. By subway: Shenyang Station (Take Line 1) → (towards Liming Square) → Qingnian Street Station (青年大街) (Exit at A) → Transfer to Line 2 (towards Quanyun Road) → Yingpan Street Station (营盘街) (Exit B) → Walk 498m to the hotel.

Shenyang North Railway station

1.Take a taxi: 12.6 km from the hotel, approximately 26 minutes.
2. By subway: Shenyangbei Station (Take Line 2) → (towards Quanyun Road) → Yingpan Street Station (营盘街) (Exit B) → walk 498 meters to the hotel.

Taoxian International Airport

1. Take a taxi: 16 km from the hotel, approximately 15 minutes
2. By subway: Taoxian International Airport (Take Line 2) → (towards Putian Road) → Yingpan Street Station (营盘街) (Exit B) → walk 498 meters to the hotel.
