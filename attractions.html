The hosting city: Jinhua

Jinhua is centrally located in Zhejiang Province, within the Yangtze River Delta Economic Zone. As the fourth largest city in Zhejiang, Jinhua is one of the wealthiest and fastest-growing areas in mainland China, having been named one of "China's Top Ten Livable Cities" for three consecutive years. Jinhua boasts a rich historical and cultural heritage and is recognized as a national historical and cultural city. It is also a modern garden city, characterized by a good ecological environment, green spaces, cleanliness, brightness, civility, orderliness, and a high quality of life.

    Hosting Institution

    The organizing and hosting institution: Zhejiang Normal University

    The conference will be organized and hosted by the School of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang Normal University. Established in 1956 as Hangzhou Normal College and renamed in 1962, Zhejiang Normal University is a comprehensive provincial key university with a focus on teacher education. In 2015, it was selected as one of the first batch of key universities in Zhejiang Province.

    The university comprises three campuses: Jinhua Campus (the main campus), Hangzhou Xiaoshan Campus, and Lanxi Campus, along with 24 colleges, including independent colleges. It boasts nearly 29,000 full-time undergraduate students, over 9,500 graduate students, and more than 3,000 international students. Zhejiang Normal University offers 70 undergraduate programs, including 34 national first-class programs and 13 provincial first-class programs. It also has 11 doctoral programs in first-level disciplines, 30 master's programs in first-level disciplines, 2 national "111 Plan" discipline innovation and intelligence bases, and 7 disciplines, including mathematics, chemistry, and computer science, ranked in the top 1% of ESI worldwide. Additionally, 20 disciplines are recognized as first-class disciplines in Zhejiang Province.

    The School of Computer Science and Technology (School of Artificial Intelligence) at Zhejiang Normal University was established in December 2022. It comprises three departments: the Department of Computer Science, the Department of Software Engineering, and the Department of Intelligent Science. The school has a postdoctoral research mobile station and a doctoral program in Computer Science and Technology.

    Zhejiang Normal University aims to achieve specialization, internationalization, regionalization, and coordination in its educational goals. The university welcomes international events and initiatives to enhance its research capabilities, foster networks and collaborations, and develop joint projects with international researchers. Hosting DASFAA2025 is expected to significantly contribute to these.
